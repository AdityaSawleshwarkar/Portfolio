WORKSHOP



On 24th June of 2023 a workshop was organised by MIT AOE on behalf of Emerging Technologies Drone Segment in order to demonstrate flying and assembly of drones and also the students had a overview of the overall calibiration of drones.








In culmination the students had a comprehensive understanding of the parts of drones as well as they were able to understand how this Emerging Technology will affect our future and also help in our future endevours
